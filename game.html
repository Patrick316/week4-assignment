Crystal Collector!

  • You will be giving a random at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a certain number points to your total score.
  • You win by matching to the random number to the box above the crystals. You loose if your number goes over.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden to you until you click on it.
  • Each new game the numbers will change.
